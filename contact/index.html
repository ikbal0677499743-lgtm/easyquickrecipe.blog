Common Questions

Can I share your recipes?

Yes! Please include a link back to the original recipe and credit Easy Quick Recipe. You can share one photo with attribution, but please don't republish the full recipe.

Can you make a recipe vegetarian/gluten-free?

I include substitution notes when I can! If you don't see one, send me an email and I'll do my best to suggest modifications.

My recipe didn't turn out right. Help!

I'm sorry to hear that! Email me with details about what happened and I'll help troubleshoot. Photos are super helpful if you have them.

Where are you located?

I'm based in Austin, Texas! I cook and test all recipes in my home kitchen here.

How do you come up with recipes?

Everywhere! Family favorites passed down from my grandmother, dishes I've simplified from restaurants, reader requests, and whatever my kids are willing to eat that week.
